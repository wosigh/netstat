About Netstat 1.0.5:
This is Netstat 1.0.5.

Requires "Netstat Daemon" version 1.0.5.

This app displays the traffic amounted on the wifi, wan and bluetooth interfaces during the current month. It requires a daemon to be installed for this: org.daemon.de.netstatd.

The day when the accounting period restarts can be tuned. Counters will be reset to zero on that day. The default is to reset counters on the 1st of every month.

If your accounting period starts on 29th - 31th of a month, set it to 1.
